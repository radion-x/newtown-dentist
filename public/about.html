Meet Our Newtown Dental Team

Dr Angelo Economos

Principal Dentist

With nearly three decades of experience in dentistry, Dr Angelo Economos has dedicated his career to delivering high-quality dental care with a focus on restorative, cosmetic, and implant dentistry. Graduating with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery with Honours from the University of Sydney, he has built a reputation for his precision, expertise, and commitment to patient-centred care.

Dr Economos continuously expands his knowledge through advanced education courses, both in Australia and internationally, ensuring he stays ahead of the latest innovations in modern dental techniques and technology. His approach blends technical skill with artistry, helping patients achieve functional, natural-looking, and long-lasting smiles.

Education & Experience

  • Bachelor of Dental Surgery (Honours) – University of Sydney
  • Over 30 years of experience in general, cosmetic, and implant dentistry
  • Advanced training in clear aligner therapy (Invisalign) and digital smile design
  • Specialist training in dental implants, oral surgery, and reconstructive dentistry
  • Certified provider of anti-wrinkle injections and dermal fillers for facial aesthetics
  • Ongoing professional development through national and international courses

Known for his gentle approach, patience, and warm personality, Dr Economos ensures that every patient feels comfortable and informed throughout their treatment. His ability to explain complex procedures in a clear and reassuring manner builds trust and confidence, allowing patients to make well-informed decisions about their oral health.

Outside of dentistry, Dr Economos enjoys spending time with his family and staying active through his love for soccer and golf, both as a player and an avid supporter. Fluent in Greek and English, he enjoys connecting with a diverse range of patients, ensuring that everyone feels at home in his care.
