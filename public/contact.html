Find Us in Newtown

We're conveniently located on King Street, Newtown – right in the heart of Sydney's Inner West. Easy access by public transport or street parking available.

By Train

Newtown Station is just a 5-minute walk from our clinic. We're easily accessible from the Inner West Line.

By Bus

Multiple bus routes stop along King Street. Routes 352, 370, 422, 423, 426, 428 all service the area.

Parking

Street parking is available on King Street and surrounding side streets. Please check local signage for time limits.

Dental Emergency?

If you're experiencing a dental emergency, please call us immediately. We reserve appointments each day for emergency cases and will do our best to see you as soon as possible.
