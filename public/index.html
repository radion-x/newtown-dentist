Quality Care for Everyone

At Dental on King, we've cultivated a warm, friendly atmosphere to help you feel relaxed throughout your visit. Our blend of modern technology and skilled professionals ensures your smile stays healthy and radiant. Experience accessible, personalised dental care tailored to your unique needs.

A Comfortable Experience

We believe visiting the dentist should be a pleasant experience for everyone. Our welcoming space is designed with your comfort as the priority, transforming routine dental care into a relaxing visit. From the moment you arrive, you'll feel at ease in our contemporary practice.

Efficient and Respectful Care

We understand your time is valuable, which is why we prioritise prompt and efficient dental services without compromising quality. Our team focuses on delivering comprehensive care tailored to your requirements, ensuring you receive exceptional treatment in a timely manner.

Conveniently Located in Newtown

Situated on bustling King Street in Newtown, our clinic is easily accessible via public transport, with on-street parking available nearby. We're delighted to welcome you to our neighbourhood practice, making it simpler than ever to prioritise your oral health.

Modern Technology

We invest in the latest dental technology to ensure precise diagnoses and comfortable treatments. From digital x-rays to intraoral cameras, we provide state-of-the-art care.

Emergency Appointments

Dental emergencies can happen at any time. We offer same-day appointments for urgent cases to relieve pain and address immediate dental concerns quickly and effectively.
