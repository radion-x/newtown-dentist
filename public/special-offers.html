Keep Your Smile for Life

Caring for your teeth isn't just about aesthetics—it's essential for overall wellness and long-term health. From daily brushing and flossing to regular checkups, small steps can have a huge impact on your confidence and quality of life. Invest in your smile today and enjoy a future full of happy, healthy grins.

  • Brush Twice a Day: Aim for at least two minutes each time to remove plaque effectively
  • Floss Daily: Clean between teeth to reach spots your toothbrush can't
  • Schedule Regular Check-Ups: Early detection and prevention go a long way
  • Limit Sugary Foods & Drinks: Reducing sugar helps protect against cavities
  • Stay Hydrated: Drinking water rinses away food particles and bacteria
What can I expect from my appointment?

During your appointment at Dental on King, our highly experienced dentists will assess your mouth, teeth, gums, and other surrounding tissues. This may involve the use of photographs and x-rays if necessary. Should any issues be identified, the dentist will discuss these with you and outline suitable treatment options. Following this, our dental hygienist will perform a comprehensive clean. Your teeth will be scaled and polished to remove any tartar above and below the gumline. Any infection present will be addressed, and decay-preventing treatments may be applied if needed.

How often do I need to have my teeth cleaned?

The frequency of professional teeth cleaning depends on how well you care for your teeth. Consistent brushing, flossing, avoiding sugary foods and drinks, and not smoking all contribute to better oral health. However, we understand maintaining perfect habits every day can be challenging. That's why we recommend scheduling a professional clean at least once every six months to ensure your oral health remains in top condition.
