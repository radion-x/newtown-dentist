Dental Bridges

Bridge the gap left by missing teeth with natural-looking, permanent dental bridges.

Replace Missing Teeth Seamlessly

A dental bridge is a fixed prosthetic device used to replace one or more missing teeth by anchoring artificial teeth to the adjacent natural teeth or implants. The result is a seamless restoration that fills the gap and restores your smile.

At Dental on King, we create custom bridges using premium materials that match your natural teeth in colour and appearance. Our bridges are designed for both optimal function and aesthetics.

Missing teeth can affect your ability to chew, speak clearly, and smile confidently. They can also cause adjacent teeth to shift over time. A dental bridge addresses all these concerns while providing a long-lasting solution.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a dental bridge last?

With proper care, dental bridges typically last 10-15 years or even longer. Good oral hygiene and regular dental visits are essential for longevity.

Is a bridge better than an implant?

Both have advantages. Bridges are faster to complete and don't require surgery. Implants preserve adjacent teeth. We'll recommend the best option for your situation.

How do I clean under my bridge?

Special floss threaders or interdental brushes help clean under the bridge. We'll show you the proper technique at your appointment.

Does getting a bridge hurt?

Local anaesthesia ensures you're comfortable during the procedure. Any post-treatment sensitivity is usually mild and short-lived.
