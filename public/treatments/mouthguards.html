Sports Mouthguards

Protect your smile during sports with custom-fitted mouthguards.

Essential Protection for Active People

Dental injuries are common in contact sports and activities with a risk of falls or collisions. A properly fitted mouthguard is one of the best ways to protect your teeth, lips, tongue, and jaw during physical activity.

At Dental on King, we create custom mouthguards that are precisely fitted to your teeth. Unlike store-bought options, custom mouthguards offer superior protection, comfort, and fit – so you'll actually wear them.

Whether you're playing rugby, AFL, basketball, hockey, martial arts, or any sport with a risk of contact, a custom mouthguard is a small investment to protect your smile from potentially costly and painful injuries.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why not just buy a mouthguard from a shop?

Store-bought mouthguards offer limited protection and are uncomfortable. They're often bulky, make breathing difficult, and tend not to be worn consistently.

How long does a custom mouthguard last?

With proper care, a custom mouthguard typically lasts 2-3 seasons. Children may need replacements more often as their mouths grow.

How do I care for my mouthguard?

Rinse after use, brush with toothpaste occasionally, store in a ventilated case, and keep away from heat which can warp the material.

Do I need a mouthguard for non-contact sports?

Consider a mouthguard for any activity with a risk of falls or contact – skateboarding, cycling, gymnastics, and similar activities.

Can I wear a mouthguard with braces?

Yes! We can make custom mouthguards designed to fit over braces, providing essential protection during orthodontic treatment.

How quickly can I get a custom mouthguard?

Usually within 1-2 weeks. If you need one urgently for an upcoming season, let us know and we'll do our best to accommodate.
