Periodontics

Specialised care for your gums and the supporting structures of your teeth.

Advanced Gum Care

Periodontics is the dental specialty focused on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases affecting the gums and supporting structures of the teeth. Healthy gums are essential for maintaining healthy teeth throughout your life.

At Dental on King, we provide comprehensive periodontal care ranging from preventive treatments to managing advanced gum disease. Our approach combines thorough assessment, effective treatment, and patient education to achieve optimal gum health.

Whether you need routine periodontal maintenance, deep cleaning, or more advanced treatment, we have the expertise and technology to help you achieve and maintain healthy gums.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a regular clean and periodontal treatment?

Regular cleaning removes deposits above the gumline. Periodontal treatment (scaling and root planing) removes deposits below the gumline and smooths root surfaces.

Is periodontal treatment painful?

Local anaesthesia is used to ensure comfort. Most patients experience minimal discomfort during and after treatment.

How often will I need maintenance visits?

After active treatment, most periodontal patients benefit from maintenance visits every 3-4 months to maintain results.

Can gum disease be cured?

Early gum disease (gingivitis) can be reversed. Advanced periodontitis can be controlled and managed but requires ongoing care.
