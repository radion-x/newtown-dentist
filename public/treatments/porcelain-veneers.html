Porcelain Veneers

Achieve the smile you've always dreamed of with custom-crafted porcelain veneers.

Transform Your Smile

Porcelain veneers are ultra-thin shells of ceramic material bonded to the front surface of teeth to create a beautiful, natural-looking smile. They're an excellent solution for addressing multiple cosmetic concerns in one treatment.

At Dental on King, we use premium porcelain to create veneers that mimic the light-reflecting properties of natural teeth. Each veneer is custom-designed to complement your facial features and desired outcome.

Whether you want to correct chips, close gaps, reshape teeth, or achieve a brighter smile, veneers offer a versatile and long-lasting solution. Many of our patients describe their veneer transformation as life-changing.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do porcelain veneers last?

With proper care, porcelain veneers typically last 10-20 years. Avoiding hard foods and maintaining good oral hygiene helps extend their lifespan.

Do veneers look natural?

Yes. Quality porcelain veneers are virtually indistinguishable from natural teeth. They're custom-made to match your desired shade and reflect light naturally.

Can veneers stain?

Porcelain is highly stain-resistant, much more so than natural teeth. However, the bonding material at the edges can discolour, so good hygiene is important.

Is the procedure painful?

The procedure is performed under local anaesthesia and is generally comfortable. Most patients report minimal sensitivity afterward.
