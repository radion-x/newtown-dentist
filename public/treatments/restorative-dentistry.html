Restorative Dentistry

Restore the function, health, and beauty of your smile. Expert solutions for damaged or missing teeth.

Reclaim Your Smile and Confidence

Damaged or missing teeth can affect more than just your appearance—they can impact your ability to eat, speak, and enjoy life to the fullest. At Dental on King, we specialize in restorative dentistry to bring your smile back to optimal health and function.

Our restorative treatments are designed to repair or replace teeth that have been affected by decay, trauma, or disease. We use the latest materials and techniques to ensure that your restorations are durable, comfortable, and look completely natural.

Whether you need a simple filling, a crown to protect a weakened tooth, or a comprehensive solution for missing teeth like implants or bridges, Dr. Angelo Economos and our team are here to provide personalized care tailored to your needs.
