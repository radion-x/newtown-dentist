Smile Makeovers

Complete smile transformations that combine multiple cosmetic treatments for stunning, natural-looking results.

Transform Your Smile Completely

A smile makeover is a comprehensive approach to improving your smile using a combination of cosmetic dental procedures. Rather than addressing one issue at a time, we create a complete treatment plan that addresses all your aesthetic concerns together.

Every smile makeover is unique, tailored to your specific goals, facial features, and dental needs. Whether you want to correct discolouration, repair chips and cracks, straighten misaligned teeth, or completely reshape your smile, we'll design a personalised plan to achieve your dream result.

Dr Angelo Economos combines over 30 years of experience with an artistic eye to create beautiful, natural-looking smiles that enhance your appearance and boost your confidence.

Treatments That May Be Included

  • Porcelain Veneers

  • Teeth Whitening

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a smile makeover take?

Timeline varies depending on the treatments involved. Simple makeovers may take a few weeks; complex cases involving implants may take several months. We'll provide a clear timeline during your consultation.

How much does a smile makeover cost?

Cost depends on the treatments included in your plan. We'll provide a detailed quote during your consultation and discuss payment options including interest-free plans.

Will my new smile look natural?

Absolutely. We take great care to design smiles that look natural and suit your face. Modern materials and techniques allow us to create remarkably lifelike results.

How do I maintain my new smile?

Good oral hygiene and regular checkups will keep your smile looking beautiful for years. We'll provide specific care instructions based on your treatments.

Can I see what my new smile will look like beforehand?

Yes! We use digital smile design and can show you a preview of your potential results during your consultation, so you know what to expect.
