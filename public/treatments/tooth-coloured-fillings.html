Tooth Coloured Fillings

Natural-looking composite resin fillings that blend seamlessly with your teeth for invisible cavity repair.

Modern, Mercury-Free Restorations

Gone are the days of unsightly silver amalgam fillings. At Dental on King, we use advanced composite resin materials that match the natural colour of your teeth, providing restorations that are virtually invisible.

Tooth coloured fillings not only look better but also bond directly to your tooth structure, providing excellent support and requiring less removal of healthy tooth material compared to traditional amalgam fillings.

Whether you need a new filling or want to replace old metal fillings, our composite restorations offer a beautiful, durable, and mercury-free solution.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do tooth coloured fillings last?

With proper care, composite fillings typically last 7-10 years or longer. Their lifespan depends on the size and location of the filling and your oral hygiene habits.

Are tooth coloured fillings as strong as amalgam?

Modern composite materials are highly durable and suitable for most fillings. For very large restorations on back teeth, we may recommend other options like inlays or crowns.

Can you replace my old silver fillings?

Yes! Many patients choose to replace old amalgam fillings with tooth coloured alternatives for aesthetic reasons. We can assess your existing fillings and discuss your options.

Is the procedure painful?

The procedure is performed under local anaesthetic, so you won't feel pain during treatment. Any post-procedure sensitivity is usually mild and temporary.
